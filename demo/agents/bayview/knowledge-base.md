# Bayview Realty — Knowledge Base

## Fee Structure

### Commission
- **Standard residential commission:** 3.95% on the first $400,000 + 2.0% on the balance (+ GST)
- **Example:** $1,200,000 sale = $15,800 + $16,000 = $31,800 + GST = $36,570 total
- **Premium properties (over $2M):** Negotiable — typically 2.5–3.0% flat (+ GST)
- **Commission is success-based** — no sale, no fee
- **Payment:** Due on unconditional settlement

### Quick Valuation Factors
When someone asks what their property might be worth, consider:
- **Location & suburb** — use the median table as a baseline
- **Land size** — larger sections in premium suburbs command significant premiums
- **Condition** — renovated vs original can be 15–25% difference
- **Views** — sea/harbour views add 10–20% in coastal suburbs
- **School zones** — Westlake, Takapuna Grammar zones particularly valued
- **Recent comparable sales** — reference the "Recently Sold" list when relevant

**Always frame estimates as indicative:** "Based on recent sales in [suburb], properties like yours are typically selling in the $X–$Y range — but a proper appraisal with one of our agents on-site would give you a much more accurate picture."

## Conjunction Policy

### What is Conjunction?
When an external agent brings a qualified buyer to a Bayview listing. The commission is split between the listing agent and the introducing agent.

### Bayview Conjunction Terms
- **Standard conjunction split:** 50/50 on the selling side commission
- **Conditions:**
  - Introducing agent must register the buyer **in writing** before any viewing or contact
  - Buyer must not already be registered with Bayview or have attended a Bayview open home for that property
  - Registration must include: buyer name, contact details, target property, and introducing agent's licence number
  - Introducing agent must hold a current REAA licence
- **Process:**
  1. Email conjunction request to hello@bayviewrealty.co.nz
  2. Bayview confirms registration within 24 hours
  3. Introducing agent attends viewings with their buyer (or Bayview shows on their behalf by arrangement)
  4. If the buyer purchases, commission is split per agreed terms at settlement

### What to Say to Agents Asking About Conjunction
Be professional and helpful — these are industry colleagues:
- Confirm Bayview does work in conjunction
- Share the standard 50/50 split
- Ask them to email the registration details
- Let them know which agent is handling the listing
