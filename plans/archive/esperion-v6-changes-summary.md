### 2. Financial Inconsistency — Fixed (Section 1 + Section 14)

**Problem in V5.1:** Executive Summary stated "Year 1 Target: 50 clients, $955K NZD revenue" but the detailed monthly projections showed ~$451K in actual Year 1 cash revenue. These are both correct but measuring different things — the $955K was the annualised run rate if all 50 clients had been onboard for 12 months, which isn't the operational reality.

**Fixed in V6:** Three clearly defined figures:
- **Year 1 Cash Revenue:** ~$451K NZD (actual collected during ramp)
- **End of Year 1 ARR:** ~$705K NZD (MRR × 12 at Month 12)
- **Annualised Potential:** ~$955K NZD (run-rate if all clients onboarded Month 1)

The $451K is the honest operational figure. The $705K ARR at Month 12 is the growth signal.

### 3. New Section: Legal Entity, Compliance & Insurance (Section 16)

**Entirely new section — most significant V6 addition.**

Sub-sections:
- **16.1 NZ Ltd Company registration** — why Ltd not sole trader, how to register ($105)
- **16.2 GST registration** — register from Day 1, not when hit $60K threshold
- **16.3 Shareholders' Agreement** — key clauses, recommended lawyers, budget ($2K–$5K)
- **16.4 Client Contracts** — mandatory clauses, liability cap, IP ownership
- **16.5 NZ Privacy Act 2020 compliance** — principles, practical rules for delivery
- **16.6 Health Information Privacy Code** — medical/dental vertical compliance boundary (scheduling only, not clinical data)
- **16.7 Insurance** — Professional Indemnity ($1M, mandatory before first client), Public Liability, Cyber Liability, plus CVE-2026-25253 deployment security guidance
- **16.8 RBP Network registration** — how to become an approved provider, timeline, benefit

**Why this was added:** A professional services firm without PI insurance, proper contracts, and Privacy Act compliance is a liability. One client claim or privacy breach could end the business. These protections cost $7K–$16K in Year 1 and are already factored into the financial model.

### 4. Competitive Landscape — Jess Mason / NovaSoft AI Added (Section 7)

**The most significant market intelligence update in V6.**

Jess Mason / NovaSoft AI runs `openclawconsultant.com` — offering OpenClaw discovery sessions, full deployment on Mac Mini/VPS, training, and optimization. This is the first identified direct competitor doing exactly what Esperion plans.

**How V6 addresses this:**
