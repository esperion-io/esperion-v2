*📣 MARKETING CHANNEL ANALYSIS*

Ranked by ROI for our specific business:

*1. Direct outbound calling — ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (trades)*
- Non-negotiable for trades. Tradies don't read LinkedIn. They answer their phone.
- CAC: $200–$400 (time only). LTV:CAC = 26:1–52:1.
- Make 20–30 calls/day. Connect rate 30–40%. Demo conversion 15–20%.

*2. Referral partners — ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (all verticals)*
- One trade accountant with 50 trade clients = $400K+ in pipeline
- Referral fee: 10% setup + 5% Year 1 retainer
- Higher quality leads than any paid channel

*3. LinkedIn organic — ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (RE, recruitment, PM)*
- 1.4M NZ professionals on LinkedIn
- Post 3x/week: numbers-first results stories, no fluff
- Free. Start now.

*4. Facebook/Meta Ads — ⭐⭐⭐ (trades awareness)*
- NZ average CPL: $42.14 (all industries), $100–$150+ for B2B services
- Good for top-of-funnel awareness and retargeting
- *Facebook Marketplace = C2C only. NOT for B2B services.*
- Activate Month 3 at $300–500/month

*5. LinkedIn Ads — ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (RE/recruitment principals)*
- 1.4M NZ professionals, 4 in 5 drive business decisions
- CPL: $150–$400 NZD — expensive but quality leads
- Only activates after case studies exist (Month 4+)

*6. Google Ads — ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (all, Month 3+)*
- CPC: $3–8 NZD for AI-specific keywords
- CPL estimate: $80–$200 NZD
- Best for high-intent searches ("AI for plumbers NZ", "AI answering service NZ")
- Start at $500/month with case study ad copy
