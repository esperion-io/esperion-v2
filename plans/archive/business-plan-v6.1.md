## 13. Revenue Model & Unit Economics (Detailed Cost Breakdown)

### Cost Absorption Policy

**Decision: Absorb all tool and model costs into the retainer margin. Do NOT pass through to clients.**

**Reasoning:**
1. **Cleaner pricing** — Clients pay a fixed monthly fee. No invoice surprises.

### 16.5 NZ Privacy Act 2020 Compliance

**The NZ Privacy Act 2020 replaced the 1993 Act and has real teeth.** Breaches can result in fines up to $10,000 for individuals and reputational damage. For a trust-dependent consultancy, a privacy incident is existential.

**Core obligations (apply to Esperion and our clients):**

| Principle | What It Means for Esperion |
|-----------|---------------------------|
| Purpose limitation | Only collect personal data needed for the stated purpose. Client customer data goes into AI systems only as needed to deliver the service. |
| Transparency | Clients' customers must know their data is being processed by AI. Include disclosure language in client onboarding. |
| Data minimisation | Don't store client customer data in Esperion systems. Data stays in client's own CRM/practice management system. Our AI reads it; doesn't archive it. |
| Security safeguards | Reasonable security measures for all data we handle. Covered by OpenClaw's security model and encryption in transit. |
| Access and correction | If asked, Esperion can provide information on what data we've processed. |

**Practical rules for delivery:**
- Client customer data (names, phone numbers, emails) processed through our AI systems for automation purposes — this is permitted under Privacy Act with client consent
- Never store raw patient/client personal data in Esperion's own databases
- All AI configurations use data that lives in the client's own systems (Cliniko, Rex, ServiceM8, etc.)
- Our contracts include a data processing addendum — we process data on behalf of the client, not for our own purposes

- [ ] Vapi voice agent scripts do NOT collect or reference health information ("I'm calling to confirm your appointment with Auckland Dental Centre on Tuesday at 2pm" — this is permitted)

**The safe boundary:** If it's about scheduling a time, it's fine. If it involves why the appointment is scheduled (diagnosis, condition, procedure), it stays in the practice management system and does not flow through our stack.

**Minimum coverage:** $500K NZD per claim

**Get PI before signing the first client. This is not negotiable.**

**Coverage:** $1M–$2M NZD
**Estimated annual premium:** $500–$1,000 NZD/year
**Can be bundled with PI** — ask for a package.
