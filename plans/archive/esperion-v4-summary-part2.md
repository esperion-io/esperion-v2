**🎯 Vertical Strategy — Dual Launch with Pivot Guardrails**

Two verticals from Day 1. Market signal determines primary focus — not predetermined.

- **Anthony → Real Estate:** Warm LinkedIn outreach to existing contacts. Mid-size agencies. Tier 2 ($5K setup + $1,250/mo). Longer cycle, higher value.
- **Co-founder → Trades:** Cold calling Auckland plumbers/electricians. Tier 1 ($2.5K setup + $625/mo). Faster close, higher volume.

**Day 45 Traction Check — mandatory:**
- Both ≥5 clients → continue dual vertical
- One ≥5, other <2 → redirect all resources to winner
- Both <5 → consolidate to one vertical immediately (follow data, no ego)

**Other guardrails:** Max 20 clients before delivery hire is triggered. Build both demos before any outreach starts.
