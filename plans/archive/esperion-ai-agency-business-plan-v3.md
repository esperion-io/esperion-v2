- But: only **12%** have scaled AI organisation-wide

**The sweet spot for Esperion:** The 46% who are "exploratory" — they're curious, pre-sold on the concept, have budget for tech, and are frustrated they can't make it work themselves. These are not sceptics. They're believers waiting for someone who can execute.

## 16. Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: AI Tools Change Fast (HIGH probability, MEDIUM impact)
**Mitigation:** Multi-tool stack. Never single-vendor dependent. OpenClaw as orchestration layer with 2–3 substitutes for each function.

### Risk 2: Platform Vendors Build Native AI (HIGH probability, MEDIUM-HIGH impact)
**Mitigation:** We'll be entrenched in client operations before this happens. Switching costs (technical + relational) are painful. As basic AI commoditises, we move upmarket to complex OpenClaw PA configurations.

### Risk 3: Delivery Quality at Speed (HIGH risk with 2 founders going fast)
**Mitigation:** SOP library built in Week 1. First 5 clients get white-glove service — no rushing. Scale velocity only after Case Study #1 is documented and the template is proven. Don't close more than you can deliver with quality.

### Risk 4: Co-Founder Conflict / Misalignment (MEDIUM probability, HIGH impact)
**Mitigation:** Shareholders' Agreement with clear buyout provisions. Weekly founder check-ins. Clear role separation. Annual equity and compensation review. If misalignment emerges early, address it at Month 3, not Month 12.

### Risk 5: Client Churn if Results Don't Show (LOW if executed well, HIGH impact)
**Mitigation:** Only take clients where ROI is measurable. Track metrics from Day 1. Founding clients: go above and beyond. Their testimonials fund everything.

### Risk 6: Competition Intensifies (MEDIUM, 6–12 months)
**Mitigation:** Business AI Agents and User Labs are already competing. Build vertical moat NOW. Get 10 trade clients and 5 RE clients in the first 90 days with case studies attached. That depth is not catchable quickly.

### Risk 7: NZ Market Too Small
**Mitigation:** 50K+ trade businesses alone. 1,197 recruitment agencies. 2,563 dental practices. 1,000 GP practices. 906 PM companies. 11,959 RE businesses. Total addressable Tier 1–3 market: 65,000+ businesses. 180 clients = 0.3% penetration. Not a ceiling problem. AU expansion is the natural Year 3 play.

### Risk 8: OpenClaw Access/Pricing Changes (LOW)
**Mitigation:** Anthony has inside knowledge of changes. Multi-tool stack means OpenClaw is one powerful layer, not the only layer. Client relationships don't depend on one tool.

### Risk 9: NZ Economy Softness (MEDIUM — ongoing)
**Mitigation:** AI that saves $10,000–$15,000/month is recession-proof. Cost-saving and efficiency tools sell better in a soft economy. Our ROI pitch is counter-cyclical — the harder the economy, the more powerful the value proposition.
