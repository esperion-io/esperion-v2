**💰 Cost Breakdown — Full Transparency (V4 NEW)**

**Decision: ABSORB all tool/model costs. Do NOT pass through to clients.**
Cleaner pricing. Justifies the retainer. We control model selection for margin efficiency.

**Model selection strategy:**
- GPT-4o-mini ($0.15–0.30/M tokens): Chatbot, FAQ, simple routing — default
- Claude Haiku 4.5 ($1/$5 per 1M): Voice agent LLM, OpenClaw PA routine
- Claude Sonnet 4.5 ($3/$15 per 1M): Complex tasks only (reports, CV screening)
- GPT-4o: Avoid for routine — 10–20× more expensive than Haiku/mini
