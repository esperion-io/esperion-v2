Global EdTech giants are all building AI features. NZ-specific competition is thin, but international competition is heavy. Differentiation must be deep (NZ curriculum, NZ referencing formats, privacy compliance, NZ-specific career content) — not shallow.
- **Scalability (5):** Digital subscription product, zero marginal delivery cost. One institutional deal unlocks tens of thousands of users. Scale potential is the highest of any vertical — if it works. This score reflects the ceiling, not current position.

**The honest summary:** Education scores 21 — lowest of the six verticals. That's accurate. It's a different business model (product vs. service), has the toughest WTP environment, and faces heavy global competition. But it has the largest raw user pool and the highest ceiling through institutional deals. Run it as a separate product track alongside the core B2B agency, not as a replacement.

Trades and Real Estate remain the Day 1 launch verticals.

**⚠️ Compliance Note — MANDATORY for this vertical:**
NZ Privacy Act 2020 and the Health Information Privacy Code 2020 (HIPC) apply to all health data. Any AI system handling patient scheduling, reminders, or health-adjacent communications must:
- Not store or process patient health records in third-party AI systems without explicit consent

## 13. Revenue Model & Unit Economics (Detailed Cost Breakdown)

### Cost Absorption Policy

**Decision: Absorb all tool and model costs into the retainer margin (B2B). Do NOT pass through to clients.**

**Reasoning:**
1. **Cleaner pricing** — Clients pay a fixed monthly fee. No invoice surprises.
2. **Justifies the retainer** — The monthly fee covers infrastructure, continuous model improvements, and usage.
3. **Control over model selection** — When we absorb costs, we can switch models without notifying clients.
4. **Competitive positioning** — Competitors who pass through costs feel transactional.
5. **Simplicity at scale** — Fixed costs per tier = clean books.

**Risk mitigation:** Include a usage cap clause in contracts. If a client's Vapi usage exceeds 500 min/month (Tier 1) or 700 min/month (Tier 2), a usage review is triggered.

**Rule:** Never use GPT-4o or Claude Sonnet for tasks that GPT-4o-mini or Haiku can handle well. The cost difference is 10–20×.
